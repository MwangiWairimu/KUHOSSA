POLICY STATEMENTS

General Policy Statement

Our general policy statement succinctly conveys the mission, values, and guiding principles of our Occupational Health and Safety (OSH) club. It provides a clear and inspiring overview of what our club aims to achieve and how it intends to operate.

Mission

Our mission is promote and advance the principles of Occupational Health and safety ( OSH) by fostering a community of dedicated individuals committed to safety,well-being, and professional growth. We strive to create a culture of safety awareness, empower our members with knowledge and skills, and make a positive impact on our campus and wider community.

Vision

Our vision is to be a leading student organization that prepares its members to become future safety leaders and advocates. We envision a world where safety is a top priority in all aspects of life, and our club plays a pivotal role in this vision.

Core Values

  • Safety: We prioritize safety in all our activities and decisions, recognizing that the well-being of individuals is paramount.
  • Professionalism: We uphold the highest standards of professionalism, ethics, and integrity in our actions and interactions.
  • Education: We are committed to continuous learning and knowledge sharing, promoting excellence in the field of Occupational Health and Safety.
  • Community: We foster a welcoming and inclusive community that values diversity, collaboration, and mutual support.
  • Leadership: We nurture leadership skills and encourage our members to take initiative and inspire others.
  • Impact: We measure our success by the positive impact we make on our campus, community, and the future of Occupational Health and Safety.

Guiding Principles

  • We embrace the principles of safety and Occupational Health and Safety in all aspects of our club's activities.
  • We provide opportunities for personal and professional growth through education, training, and hands-on experience.
  • We advocate for safety awareness and best practices within our campus community and beyond.
  • We value the unique contributions of each member and promote an inclusive and collaborative environment.
  • We uphold the highest ethical standards and act as responsible stewards of club resources.

Commitment

As members of KUOHSSA, we commit ourselves to these values and principles. We pledge to work together to achieve our mission, support one another in our Occupational Health and Safety journey, and continuously strive for excellence.
