STRATEGIC AGENDA

1. Safety Advocacy Campaigns

Develop and execute campaigns to raise awareness about safety issues on campus or in the community. Focus on ergonomics, mental health, fire safety, and workplace safety.

2. Guest Speaker Series

Organize regular talks and seminars with industry experts, OSH professionals, and alumni to share their insights and experiences.

3. Training and Workshops

Offer hands-on workshops on OSH topics such as first aid, risk assessment, and safety protocol implementation.

4. Certification Support

Assist members in acquiring safety certifications by providing resources, study groups, and exam preparation materials.

5. Collaboration with Industry

Partner with local businesses and organizations to provide internship opportunities, site visits, and real-world OSH experience.

6. Community Outreach

Engage in outreach programs like school visits, safety fairs, or volunteering for disaster relief efforts to promote safety awareness.

7. Research and Publications

Encourage members to undertake OSH-related research and publish findings in journals or present at conferences.

8. Networking Events

Host events that allow members to connect with OSH professionals, potential employers, and alumni.

9. Mentorship Program

Establish a mentorship system where experienced members provide guidance and support to newcomers in their OSH journey.

10. Safety Audits

Conduct safety audits on campus or in the community to identify hazards and recommend improvements.

11. Collaborative Projects

Collaborate with other student organizations or departments on interdisciplinary projects that promote safety and well-being.

12. Emergency Response Training

Provide CPR and first aid certification training sessions to equip members with life-saving skills.

13. Annual Conferences or Symposiums

Organize an annual OSH conference or symposium where members can present research, attend workshops, and network with professionals.
