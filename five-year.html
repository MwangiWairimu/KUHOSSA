Strategic Five-Year Plan (2023-2028)

Mission Statement:

To promote safety, well- being, and professional growth among our members, while fostering a culture of safety awareness on campus and beyond.

Vision Statement:

To become a leading student organization that prepares members for successful careers in Occupational Health and Safety, while making a positive impact on our community

Year 1: Building Foundations (2023/2024)

Goal 1: Increase Club Membership and Engagement

  • Recruit a minimum of 50 new members.
  • Host monthly member meetings and social events to foster engagement.

Goal 2: Educational Initiatives

  • Conduct at least six workshops or training sessions on key OSH topics.
  • Establish a mentorship program pairing experienced members with newcomers.

Year 2: Outreach and Awareness (2024/2025)

Goal 3: Community Engagement

  • Collaborate with local schools to deliver safety education programs.
  • Organize a safety fair or event open to the campus and local community.

Goal 4: Industry Partnerships

  • Establish partnerships with at lest two local businesses for internship or site visits.
  • Invite OSH professionals to speak at club events.

Year 3: Skill Enhancement (2025/2026)

Goal 5: Certification Support

  • Provide resources and support for members seeking OSH certifications.
  • Host a certification exam preparation workshop.

Goal 6: Research and Publications

  • Encourage and fund at least two research projects by club members.
  • Publish findings in a campus or external OSH journal.

Year 4: Leadership Development (2026/2027)

Goal 7: Leadership Training

  • Provide leadership training for the executive team and committee leaders.
  • Implement a leadership development program for all interested members.

Goal 8: Networking Opportunities

  • Organize a regional OSH conference or symposium.
  • Establish partnerships with other student organizations for collaborative projects.

Year 5: Impact and Sustainability (2027/2028)

Goal 9: Community Safety Initiatives

  • Conduct safety audits on campus and propose improvements.
  • Engage in at least one major community safety project.

Goal 10: Ongoing Evaluation

  • Evaluate the success of the strategic plan and gather feedback from members.
  • Use feedback to create a new five-year plan that builds on achievements.
